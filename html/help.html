Instructions

  1. Add Player1 and Player2 names.
  2. Pick how many Pokemon you want to battle with (3 or 6).
  3. Click submit. Each player will receive 3 or 6 random Pokemon.
  4. Hover over a Pokemon to see its stats.
  5. Click on a Pokemon to choose it for battle and confirm. The 'Ready' button will change to green.
  6. Once both players have chosen their Pokemon, click 'Battle'.
  7. Stats will update. Continue steps 5 and 6 until one player has no Pokemon left.
  8. Click 'Restart' to start a new game.
